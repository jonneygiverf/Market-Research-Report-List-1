<p><p>Terrazzo Tile is divided into two market types: Inorganic Terrazzo Tile and Epoxy Terrazzo Tile. Inorganic Terrazzo Tile is made from natural materials like marble, granite, and quartz, mixed with cement. It offers durability, low maintenance, and a beautiful appearance. On the other hand, Epoxy Terrazzo Tile is created by mixing epoxy resin with colored glass, marble, or other aggregates. It provides a high-gloss finish, is more flexible in design, and offers better chemical resistance. Both types of Terrazzo Tiles are widely used in various architectural projects for their unique qualities and aesthetics.</p></p>
