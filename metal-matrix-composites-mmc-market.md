The market for MMC is categorized into several types. Aluminum MMC involves combining aluminum with reinforcing particles like ceramic or carbon fibers. Nickel MMC uses nickel as the matrix material, often reinforced with boron or carbide particles. Refractory MMC utilizes refractory metals such as tungsten or molybdenum with reinforcing materials. Other types include magnesium MMC, beryllium MMC, and titanium MMC, which involve similar combinations of respective metals with reinforcing particles.</p></p>
