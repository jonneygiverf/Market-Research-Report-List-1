<p><p>The vehicle detectors market is comprised of various types of detectors including inductive loop, ultrasonic, magnetic, radar, and others. Inductive loop detectors use a loop of wire embedded in the pavement to detect changes in inductance caused by vehicles passing over it. Ultrasonic detectors emit sound waves and measure the time it takes for the waves to bounce back, detecting the presence of vehicles. Magnetic detectors use magnetic fields to sense the presence of vehicles. Radar detectors use radio waves to determine the distance and speed of vehicles. Other types of detectors include infrared and video-based systems.</p></p>
