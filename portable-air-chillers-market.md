<p><p>Portable air chillers are cooling systems used for various industrial and commercial applications. Scroll chillers are compact and use a scroll compressor to provide cooling, making them energy-efficient and quiet. They are suitable for smaller cooling loads. Screw chillers, on the other hand, are larger systems that use a screw compressor to provide cooling. They are more powerful and suitable for larger cooling loads. Both types of chillers are portable, meaning they can be easily moved and installed in different locations as needed.</p></p>
