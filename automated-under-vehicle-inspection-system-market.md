<p><p>The Automated Under Vehicle Inspection System (AUVIS) market is divided into two main types: fixed and mobile. They provide continuous monitoring and inspection of vehicles passing through those areas. On the other hand, mobile AUVIS systems are portable and can be deployed quickly in various locations. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing security measures and ensuring effective vehicle inspection.</p></p>
