<p><p>The rotor type plastic granulator market includes two main types: horizontal type and vertical type. Horizontal type plastic granulators are designed with a horizontal cutting chamber and a rotor that rotates horizontally. They are commonly used for processing larger plastic items and offer high throughput. On the other hand, vertical type plastic granulators have a vertical cutting chamber and a rotor that rotates vertically. They are suitable for processing smaller plastic materials and provide more compact size for space-constrained environments. These two types cater to different needs in the plastic granulation industry.</p></p>
