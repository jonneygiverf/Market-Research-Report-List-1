<p><p>The LNMO cathode market encompasses two major types: cathode sheets and cathode powder. Cathode sheets are thin layers of LNMO material, which are widely used in the production of rechargeable lithium-ion batteries. They provide a high energy density and stability to the battery. On the other hand, cathode powder refers to LNMO material that is finely ground into a powder form. This powdered LNMO is commonly used in the manufacturing of smaller electronic devices or as an intermediate material for further processing into cathode sheets.</p></p>
