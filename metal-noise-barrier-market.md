<p><p>The Metal Noise Barrier market consists of two types: Ground-Mounted Noise Barrier and Structure-Mounted Noise Barrier. Ground-Mounted Noise Barrier refers to barriers that are installed on the ground level and are designed to reduce noise pollution from roadways, highways, or railways. On the other hand, Structure-Mounted Noise Barrier refers to barriers that are attached to existing structures, such as buildings or bridges, and are used to mitigate noise from sources like industrial facilities or construction sites. Both types serve the purpose of reducing noise levels in urban areas and promoting a quieter environment.</p></p>
