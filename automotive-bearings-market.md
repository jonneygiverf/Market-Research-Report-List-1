These bearings come in various types such as tapered roller bearings, deep groove ball bearings, needle roller bearings, and others. Tapered roller bearings are designed to handle both radial and axial loads. Deep groove ball bearings are used to support radial and axial loads in high-speed applications. Needle roller bearings have long and thin cylindrical rollers, providing high load capacity in a limited space. Other types include thrust bearings, spherical roller bearings, and angular contact ball bearings, catering to specific automotive requirements.</p></p>
