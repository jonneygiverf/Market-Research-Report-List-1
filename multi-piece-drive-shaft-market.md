<p><p>The multi-piece drive shaft market consists of various types, including rigid shaft and hollow shaft. Rigid shafts are solid, one-piece shafts that are commonly used in heavy-duty applications. They provide higher torsional stiffness and minimize vibrations. On the other hand, hollow shafts are designed with a hollow core, making them lighter and more flexible, suitable for lightweight applications. Both types serve different purposes based on the specific requirements of the machinery, offering respective benefits such as stability and reduced weight.</p></p>
