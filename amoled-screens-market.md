<p><p>AMOLED (Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode) screens are divided into two main market types: AMOLED hard screen and AMOLED soft screen. The AMOLED hard screen is a rigid display that offers enhanced durability and is commonly used in smartphones, tablets, and smartwatches. On the other hand, the AMOLED soft screen is flexible and can be bent or curved, making it suitable for innovative devices like foldable smartphones and wearable gadgets. These two market types cater to different consumer demands, offering diverse options for display technology in various electronic devices.</p></p>
