<p><p>The Medical IoT Software market includes two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based software refers to applications that are hosted and accessed over the internet, allowing users to store and access data remotely. This type offers flexibility, scalability, and easy integration with other systems but requires an internet connection. On the other hand, on-premises software is installed and operated within the healthcare facility's local infrastructure. It provides full control over data and operations but requires dedicated hardware and IT maintenance. Organizations choose between these types based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
