<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The microprocessor smart card market comprises four main types, namely contact cards, contactless cards, memory cards, and CPU/MPU microprocessor multifunction cards. Contact cards require physical insertion into a card reader for communication. In contrast, contactless cards use radio frequency technology for wireless communication with the reader. Memory cards store data and do not possess processing capabilities. CPU/MPU microprocessor multifunction cards incorporate both memory and processing capabilities, allowing for various applications, such as secure access, payment, and identification.</p></p>
