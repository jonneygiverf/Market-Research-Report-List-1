<p><p>Automatic tracking cameras are advanced surveillance devices that automatically follow the movement of an object or person within a specified area. These cameras are widely used in various applications, including security and surveillance, sports broadcasting, wildlife observation, and video conferencing.</p><p>The automatic tracking cameras market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
