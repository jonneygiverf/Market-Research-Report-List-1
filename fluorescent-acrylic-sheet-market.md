<p><p>The Fluorescent Acrylic Sheet market consists of two main types: Cast Acrylic Sheet and Extruded Acrylic Sheet. Cast Acrylic Sheet is manufactured by pouring the liquid acrylic polymer into a mold and then allowing it to harden. This process creates a smooth and glossy finish. On the other hand, Extruded Acrylic Sheet is produced by pushing the acrylic polymer through rollers, resulting in a sheet with a textured surface. Both types of sheets are used in various applications, such as signage, lighting fixtures, and displays, due to their vibrant fluorescent properties.</p></p>
