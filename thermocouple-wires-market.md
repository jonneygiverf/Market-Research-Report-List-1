<p><p>Thermocouple wires are used in temperature measurement applications where a change in temperature generates a voltage difference across the wires. These wires are made of two different metal alloys that are joined together at one end and connected to a measuring instrument at the other end. The junction of the two metals creates a voltage proportional to the temperature difference between the two ends of the wires, allowing for accurate temperature monitoring.</p><p>The thermocouple wires market is expected to witness significant growth in the forecast period. One of the key factors driving this growth is the increasing demand for temperature measurement devices in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, petrochemical, and healthcare.
