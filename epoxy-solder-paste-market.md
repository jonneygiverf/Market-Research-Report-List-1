<p><p>The epoxy solder paste market is divided into two main types: conductive solder paste and insulation solder paste. Conductive solder paste is used to create electrical connections by conducting electricity through the solder joints. It is typically used in electronic devices and circuit board assemblies. On the other hand, insulation solder paste is designed to prevent electrical connections by insulating the solder joints. This is commonly used in applications where electrical isolation is required, such as in high-voltage power electronics or certain automotive components.</p></p>
