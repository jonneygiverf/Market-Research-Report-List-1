<p><strong>The Oil-less Scroll Vacuum Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-sided Scroll</li><li>Double-sided Scroll</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oil-less scroll vacuum pumps are increasingly being used in various industries for their efficient, reliable, and environmentally-friendly operation. There are two main types of oil-less scroll vacuum pumps: single-sided scroll and double-sided scroll. </p><p>The single-sided scroll vacuum pump consists of a fixed and oscillating scroll, creating crescent-shaped gas pockets that trap and compress gas. These pumps offer high pumping speeds and low ultimate pressure levels.</p><p>On the other hand, double-sided scroll vacuum pumps have two intermeshing scrolls, allowing for greater capacity and improved pumping efficiency. These pumps are suitable for larger applications that require higher volumes of gas removal.</p></p>
