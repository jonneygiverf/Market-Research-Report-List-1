<p><p>Automated on-off valves are widely used in various industries to control the flow of fluids or gases. There are different types of automated on-off valves available in the market. Angle valves are designed with a right-angle shape and are commonly used in applications where space is limited. Ball valves consist of a ball with a hole in it, which allows or restricts the flow. Control valves regulate the flow rate based on input signals. Float valves use a float mechanism to control the flow by opening or closing the valve. Other types of valves include butterfly valves, gate valves, and diaphragm valves.</p></p>
