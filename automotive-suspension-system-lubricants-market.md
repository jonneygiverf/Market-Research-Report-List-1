<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The automotive suspension system lubricants market can be categorized into two types: lubricating oil for fluid suspension and independent suspension lubricating oil. Lubricating oil for fluid suspension is used in suspensions that rely on a fluid-filled system to absorb shocks and vibrations. On the other hand, independent suspension lubricating oil is used in suspensions that allow each wheel to move independently, resulting in improved ride comfort and handling. Both types of lubricants play a crucial role in maintaining the smooth operation and longevity of automotive suspension systems.</p></p>
