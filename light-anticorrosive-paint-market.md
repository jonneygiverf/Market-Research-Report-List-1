Water-based paint is formulated using water as a medium, making it more environmentally friendly and less odorous. Powder coating involves applying a dry powder, which is then cured using heat, resulting in a highly durable and attractive finish. Each type of paint offers specific advantages and is used in various applications based on its unique properties.</p></p>
