<p><p>Bentonite cat litters are available in two main types: sodium bentonite cat litters and calcium bentonite cat litters. Sodium bentonite cat litters are made from a type of clay that is highly absorbent and forms clumps when it comes into contact with liquid. This makes it easier to clean and maintain the litter box. On the other hand, calcium bentonite cat litters are also absorbent but do not form clumps. These litters are often preferred by pet owners who prioritize a more natural option for their cats.</p></p>
