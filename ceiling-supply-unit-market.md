<p><p>The Ceiling Supply Unit (CSU) market consists of four types: fixed, fixed retractable, single arm movable, and double multi arm movable. Fixed CSUs are stationary, providing a consistent supply of medical gases and electrical connections. Fixed retractable CSUs have retractable arms allowing for flexibility in positioning. Single arm movable CSUs have a single arm that can be adjusted and moved in different directions. Double multi arm movable CSUs have multiple movable arms that can be adjusted independently, offering enhanced flexibility and maneuverability for medical equipment and accessories.</p></p>
