<p><p>Pentaerythritol tetranitrate (PETN) is a versatile explosive compound used in various industries. It is classified into two market types: sulfonitric and nitrated market. In the sulfonitric market, PETN is produced by sulfonitration, a process involving the reaction between pentaerythritol and a mixture of sulfuric and nitric acids. This market type caters to industries requiring low sensitivity explosives. In contrast, the nitrated market involves the direct nitrating of pentaerythritol, resulting in a higher sensitivity explosive. This market type is suitable for applications that demand rapid and powerful detonation.</p></p>
