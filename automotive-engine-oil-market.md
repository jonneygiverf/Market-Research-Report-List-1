<p><p>The automotive engine oil market consists of three main types: single-grade, multi-grade, and others. Single-grade oil is designed for specific temperature ranges and offers good protection at high temperatures. Multi-grade oil, on the other hand, has the ability to perform under different temperature conditions, making it suitable for year-round use. It offers better engine protection during cold starts and provides lubrication in extreme temperatures.
