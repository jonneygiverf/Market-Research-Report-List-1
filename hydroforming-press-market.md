<p><p>A hydroforming press is a hydraulic machine used in the process of hydroforming, where a metallic tube or sheet is formed into complex shapes by applying fluid pressure internally. The increasing demand for lightweight materials and complex shaped components in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing is driving the market growth. Manufacturers are incorporating features like programmable logic controllers (PLCs) and human-machine interfaces (HMIs) to optimize the forming process, ensure precise control, and improve efficiency.
