<p><ul><li>Disposable</li><li>Reusable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Surgical Laser Optical Fiber market can be categorized into two types: Disposable and Reusable. Disposable optical fibers are for single-use only and are discarded after each surgical procedure. They offer convenience, reduced risk of cross-contamination, and lower maintenance costs. On the other hand, reusable optical fibers can be sterilized and used multiple times before being replaced. Although they require proper cleaning and maintenance, they are more cost-effective in the long run. The choice between these types depends on factors such as cost considerations, infection control protocols, and specific surgical requirements.</p></p>
