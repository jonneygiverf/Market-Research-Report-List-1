<p><ul><li>Windshield Projected HUD</li><li>Combiner Projected HUD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Head Up Displays (HUD) are advanced technologies that provide important driving information directly in the driver's line of sight, ensuring safer and more convenient driving experiences. There are two main types of HUDs: Windshield Projected HUD and Combiner Projected HUD. </p><p>Windshield Projected HUDs utilize a transparent technology to project information directly onto the windshield, allowing drivers to view crucial data without diverting their gaze from the road. Combiner Projected HUDs, on the other hand, use a separate combiner panel placed in the driver's line of sight to reflect the information. Both types aim to enhance driving safety and convenience by displaying speed, navigation, and other critical details while minimizing distractions.</p></p>
