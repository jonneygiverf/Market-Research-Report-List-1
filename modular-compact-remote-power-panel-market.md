<p><p>The Modular Compact Remote Power Panel (RPP) market is classified into two types: floor-standing and wall-mounted. Floor-standing RPPs are large panels designed to be placed on the floor, offering a high power distribution capacity and ease of access. On the other hand, wall-mounted RPPs are designed to be installed on walls, taking up less space and offering a compact power distribution solution. Both types serve the same purpose of providing remote power distribution and control, but differ in their installation options and sizes to cater to different space requirements.</p></p>
