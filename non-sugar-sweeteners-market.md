<p><p>The Non-Sugar Sweeteners market can be classified into two types: Natural Non-Sugar Sweeteners and Synthetic Non-Sugar Sweeteners. Natural Non-Sugar Sweeteners are derived from sources such as stevia, monk fruit, agave, and honey. These sweeteners are considered healthier alternatives to sugar as they have fewer calories and are often deemed natural. On the other hand, Synthetic Non-Sugar Sweeteners are artificially created compounds such as aspartame, saccharin, and sucralose. These sweeteners are often used in processed foods and beverages as they provide the desired sweet taste without the added calories of sugar.</p></p>
