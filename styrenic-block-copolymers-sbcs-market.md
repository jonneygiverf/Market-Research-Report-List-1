<p><p>Styrenic Block Copolymers (SBCs) are a class of thermoplastic elastomers widely used in various industries. SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene), the most common type, exhibits good elasticity and is often used in adhesives, footwear, and asphalt modification. SIS (Styrene-Isoprene-Styrene) possesses excellent tackiness and is frequently used in hot melt adhesives and sealants. SEBS (Styrene-Ethylene-Butadiene-Styrene) combines the properties of SBS and SEPS, making it suitable for applications like soft touch grips, medical devices, and automotive components. Other types may include SEPS (Styrene-Ethylene-Propylene-Styrene) and SEEPS (Styrene-Ethylene-Ethylidene-Styrene), each offering unique characteristics for specific market needs.</p></p>
