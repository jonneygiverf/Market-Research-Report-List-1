<p><p>Dosimeters for quality assurance come in various types. TLD (thermoluminescent dosimeters) dosimeters are portable and commonly used to measure ionizing radiation. They work by recording the energy absorbed by a material when exposed to radiation. OSL (optically stimulated luminescence) dosimeters utilize aluminum oxide or carbon-doped aluminum oxide to measure radiation dose. They are known for their high sensitivity and accuracy. Apart from TLD and OSL dosimeters, there are other types available in the market, each offering unique features and benefits for accurately measuring and monitoring radiation exposure in different settings and industries.</p></p>
