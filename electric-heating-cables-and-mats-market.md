<p><p>The electric heating cables and mats market includes two main types: electric heating cables and electric heating mats. Electric heating cables refer to a type of heating system that consists of electric wires designed to generate heat when an electrical current is passed through them. These cables are commonly used for applications such as floor heating, pipe freeze protection, and roof de-icing. On the other hand, electric heating mats are flexible mats embedded with heating elements that provide localized heating for specific areas. They are often used for underfloor heating systems and warming surfaces like countertops or bathroom floors.</p></p>
