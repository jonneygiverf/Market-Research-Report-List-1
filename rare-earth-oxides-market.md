<p><p>The Rare Earth Oxides (REO) market consists of two main types: Single Rare Earth Oxides and Mixed Rare Earth Oxides. Single REO refers to individual rare earth elements that have been extracted and processed into oxide form. These oxides are used in various applications, such as catalysts and phosphors. On the other hand, Mixed REO is a combination of multiple rare earth elements in oxide form. Mixed REO finds application in industries like metallurgy, electronics, and magnets, where specific combinations of rare earth elements are required for optimal performance.</p></p>
