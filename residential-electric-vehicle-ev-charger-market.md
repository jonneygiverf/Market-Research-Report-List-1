<p><p>The residential EV charger market can be broadly categorized into two types: DC charger and AC charger. DC chargers are faster and more powerful, as they provide direct current to the EV, enabling faster charging times. They are commonly used in commercial and public areas. On the other hand, AC chargers are slower but more suitable for home charging, as they convert alternating current into direct current for the EV. Both types have their advantages and are designed to cater to different charging needs in the residential EV market.</p></p>
