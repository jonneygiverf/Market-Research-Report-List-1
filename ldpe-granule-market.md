<p><p>LDPE granules, or low-density polyethylene granules, are a commonly used synthetic resin in various industries. Autoclave Process LDPE Granules are produced using a high-pressure reactor, resulting in a relatively low melt index and good impact strength. On the other hand, Tubular Process LDPE Granules are created through a low-pressure reactor, yielding a high melt index and excellent transparency. Both types have their unique uses and advantages, with Autoclave Process LDPE Granules being suitable for packaging films, while Tubular Process LDPE Granules are ideal for manufacturing bottles and containers.</p></p>
