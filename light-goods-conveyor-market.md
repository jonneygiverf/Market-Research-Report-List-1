<p><p>The light goods conveyor market offers various types of conveyors for different applications. Drag chain conveyors are used for heavy-duty applications where items need to be pulled along a flat surface. Roller conveyors, whether driven or gravity-driven, use rollers to move goods smoothly and efficiently. Plastic conveyors are lightweight and suitable for transporting delicate items. Slat conveyors use slats or plates to carry goods and are commonly used in assembly lines. These different types cater to diverse industrial needs, providing solutions for easy and efficient material handling.</p></p>
