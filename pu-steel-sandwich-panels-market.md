<p><p>PU Steel Sandwich Panels are widely used in construction due to their high insulation properties. There are three different types based on refractory temperature. Panels with a refractory temperature of 1580℃ are suitable for general buildings. Panels with a refractory temperature of 1770℃ can be used for higher temperature applications such as industrial furnaces. For extremely high-temperature environments like steel mills or glass factories, panels with refractory temperatures above 2000℃ are necessary. These panels provide excellent insulation and structural support, making them ideal for various construction projects.</p></p>
