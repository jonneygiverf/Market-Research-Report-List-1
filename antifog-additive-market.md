The market types of antifog additives include glycerol esters, polyglycerol esters, sorbitan esters of fatty acids, ethoxylated sorbitan esters, and other types. Glycerol esters are derived from glycerol and fatty acids, while polyglycerol esters are produced by reacting polyglycerol with fatty acids. Sorbitan esters of fatty acids are obtained by esterifying sorbitol with fatty acids, and ethoxylated sorbitan esters are produced by ethoxylation of sorbitan esters. Other market types encompass additional additives used for antifog purposes.</p></p>
