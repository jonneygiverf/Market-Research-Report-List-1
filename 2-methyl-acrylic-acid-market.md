<p><p>The 2-Methyl-Acrylic Acid market can be divided into two types based on the manufacturing methods: Acetone Cyanohydrin Method and Isobutylene Oxidation Method. In the Acetone Cyanohydrin Method, 2-Methyl-Acrylic Acid is produced by reacting acetone cyanohydrin with sulfuric acid. On the other hand, in the Isobutylene Oxidation Method, 2-Methyl-Acrylic Acid is produced by oxidizing isobutylene with air or oxygen.
