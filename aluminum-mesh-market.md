<p><p>The Aluminum Mesh market comprises various types of mesh, including Square Mesh, Hexagonal Mesh, Triangular Mesh, and Twisted Mesh. Square Mesh refers to an aluminum wire mesh with square-shaped openings, allowing for maximum airflow and heat dissipation. Hexagonal Mesh features a honeycomb-like pattern, offering good durability and corrosion resistance. Triangular Mesh is characterized by triangular-shaped openings, making it suitable for applications requiring high rigidity. Lastly, Twisted Mesh comprises aluminum wires that are twisted together, providing increased strength and stability. Each type has its unique properties and applications in different industries.</p></p>
