<p><p>The automobile fuel injector market consists of two main types: gasoline injection and diesel injection. Gasoline injection systems are used in vehicles that run on gasoline fuel. They deliver precise amounts of fuel directly into the engine's intake manifold. Diesel injection systems, on the other hand, are used in diesel-powered vehicles. They distribute fuel at high pressure into the engine's combustion chamber. Both types of injectors play a vital role in ensuring efficient fuel delivery, improving combustion, and reducing emissions in their respective fuel systems.</p></p>
