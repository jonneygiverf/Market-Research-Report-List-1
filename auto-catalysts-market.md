<p><strong>The Auto Catalysts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two Way Catalyst</li><li>Three Way Catalyst</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Auto catalysts play a crucial role in reducing harmful emissions from vehicles. Two-way catalysts primarily aim to control carbon monoxide (CO) and nitrogen oxides (NOx) emissions. They are commonly used in engines that operate at lean air-to-fuel ratios. On the other hand, three-way catalysts are employed in engines that run at stoichiometric air-to-fuel ratios and are designed to simultaneously minimize CO, NOx, and unburned hydrocarbon emissions. Both types of catalysts are essential in ensuring cleaner and more environmentally friendly automotive exhausts.</p></p>
