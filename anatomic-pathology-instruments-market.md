<p><p>The Anatomic Pathology Instruments market includes various types of instruments used in pathology laboratories. These instruments aid in the diagnosis and analysis of diseases and conditions. Slide staining systems are used to apply stains to tissue samples for better visibility under a microscope. Tissue processing systems help in the preparation of tissue samples for examination. Cell processors are used to automate cell staining procedures. Microtomes are used to cut thin sections of tissue for analysis. Embedded systems help in embedding tissue samples into paraffin blocks. Coverslippers are used to add coverslips to stained slides. Other instruments include grossing stations, specimen labeling systems, and specimen tracking systems.</p></p>
