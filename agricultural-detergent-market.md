<p><p>The agricultural detergent market is categorized into three main types: acid detergent, neutral detergent, and alkaline detergent. Acid detergents are used for cleaning equipment and machinery in the agricultural industry, as they effectively remove mineral deposits and scale. Neutral detergents are generally used for cleaning delicate surfaces, such as glass or plastic, without causing any damage. Alkaline detergents are powerful cleaners and are commonly utilized for heavy-duty cleaning tasks like removing grease and soil from farm equipment and surfaces. These types of detergents provide different cleaning solutions specifically tailored to the agricultural sector.</p></p>
