<p><p>Radiant ceiling panels are used for heating purposes and can be categorized into two main types: water heating and electric heating. Water heating panels utilize hot water flowing through pipes embedded in the ceiling panels to distribute heat. On the other hand, electric heating panels generate heat through the use of electrical currents passing through conductive materials. Both types offer efficient and evenly distributed heating, providing comfort and energy savings in various settings such as residential, commercial, and industrial buildings.</p></p>
