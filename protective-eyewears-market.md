<p><ul><li>Direct Vented Protective Eyewears</li><li>Indirect Vented Protective Eyewears</li><li>Non-vented Protective Eyewears</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The protective eyewear market encompasses three main types: direct vented protective eyewear, indirect vented protective eyewear, and non-vented protective eyewear. Direct vented eyewear is designed with small openings to allow air flow and prevent fogging. Indirect vented eyewear has larger openings covered by a mesh or filter to provide indirect protection from liquids and debris. Non-vented eyewear, on the other hand, lacks any openings, providing a barrier against both liquid and solid hazards. These variations cater to different needs and preferences for eye protection in various industries and environments.</p></p>
