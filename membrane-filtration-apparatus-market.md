<p><p>The membrane filtration apparatus market includes various types of filtration technologies such as microfiltration, ultrafiltration, nanofiltration, and reverse osmosis. Microfiltration involves filtering particles in the range of 0.1 to 10 microns, while ultrafiltration uses smaller pore sizes to remove smaller particles and macromolecules. Nanofiltration is used for separating ions and molecules based on their size and charge, while reverse osmosis removes dissolved salts and other contaminants by applying pressure to a solution. These filtration technologies are widely used in water treatment, pharmaceutical, food and beverage, and other industries for purification purposes.</p></p>
