<p><p>The Synthetic Graphite Anode Powder Market consists of two types: 2R Type and 3H Type. The 2R Type refers to a graphite structure characterized by two different orientations of the carbon lattice, resulting in a higher capacity and better cycling stability. On the other hand, the 3H Type represents a graphite structure with three different orientations, offering improved capacity and energy density. Both types are used in energy storage applications, such as lithium-ion batteries, with their distinct structural properties offering specific advantages in terms of performance and durability.</p></p>
