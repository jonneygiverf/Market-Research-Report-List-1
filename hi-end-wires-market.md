<p><p>The Hi-End Wires market consists of various types of copper wires used for high-quality audio systems. OFC (oxygen free copper) wires are made by eliminating oxygen to reduce impurities, providing excellent conductivity. LCOFC (linear crystal oxygen free copper) wires have an aligned crystal structure for improved conductivity. PCOCC (oxygen-free single crystal copper) wires are made with a single crystal structure, minimizing signal loss and distortion. Super PCOCC wires are enhanced version of PCOCC wires with even better conductivity. These different types cater to the demands of audiophiles seeking the best possible audio quality.</p></p>
