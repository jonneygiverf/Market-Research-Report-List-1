<p><p>Automotive wheel alignment equipment is used to align various components of a vehicle's suspension system, particularly the wheels, to ensure they are perpendicular to the ground and parallel to each other. This not only helps in improving the overall handling and stability of the vehicle but also extends the lifespan of tires. The equipment mainly consists of sensors, cameras, and software that measure and analyze wheel angles such as camber, caster, and toe.</p><p>The global automotive wheel alignment equipment market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period. Factors driving this growth include the increasing demand for passenger vehicles, rising disposable income, and the growing automotive aftermarket.
