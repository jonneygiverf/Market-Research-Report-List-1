<p><strong>The Air Suspension Control System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capsule</li><li>Membrane</li><li>Compound</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Air Suspension Control System market includes three main types: Capsule, Membrane, and Compound systems. Capsule systems use a flexible air chamber enclosed in a rigid structure to control suspension movements. Membrane systems rely on a thin, flexible membrane that separates two air chambers to regulate suspension actions. Compound systems combine both capsule and membrane technologies to provide a more efficient and effective air suspension control. These different types offer varying benefits and applications depending on the specific needs and requirements of the vehicles or machinery using them.</p></p>
