<p><p>Weather stripping is a crucial component in maintaining the energy efficiency of doors and windows. Two popular market types include EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) and TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer) weather stripping. EPDM weather stripping is widely used for its durability, flexibility, and resistance to temperature extremes. It effectively seals gaps, preventing air leakage and reducing energy costs. On the other hand, TPE weather stripping offers similar benefits but is known for its recyclability and resistance to UV rays and chemicals. Both types enhance insulation, improve comfort, and contribute to energy savings for homes and buildings.</p></p>
