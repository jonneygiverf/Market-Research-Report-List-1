<p><p>The liquid paraffin market consists of two main types: light liquid paraffin and heavy liquid paraffin. Light liquid paraffin is a type of mineral oil that is transparent, odorless, and commonly used in cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and personal care products. It is known for its skin barrier function and moisturizing properties. On the other hand, heavy liquid paraffin is a thicker and denser form of mineral oil that is mainly used in industrial applications, such as lubricants and electrical transformers, due to its high viscosity and heat resistance.</p></p>
