<p><p>Heated Getter Gas Purifiers are devices used to remove impurities and contaminants from gases in various industries. There are two main types of these purifiers: Point of Use Gas Purifiers and Bulk Gas Purifiers. Point of Use Gas Purifiers are small units designed for specific applications and installed directly at the gas source. They efficiently purify gases at a relatively low flow rate. On the other hand, Bulk Gas Purifiers are larger systems installed at the gas supply source. They purify gases in large quantities and are commonly used in industrial processes.</p></p>
