<p><p>The Vehicle Grade LiDAR Sensor market consists of two main types: Mechanical LiDAR and Solid State LiDAR. Mechanical LiDAR sensors typically use a rotating mechanism to emit laser pulses and measure their reflections, providing high resolution and long-range detection capabilities. On the other hand, Solid State LiDAR sensors are based on stationary components, such as MEMS mirrors or optical phased arrays, allowing for smaller form factors and lower costs. Both types are essential in enabling autonomous vehicles with accurate and real-time perception capabilities.</p></p>
