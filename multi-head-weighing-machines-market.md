<p><p>Multi-head weighing machines are used in various industries to accurately weigh and package products. Linear multi-head weighing machines are designed with linear feeding systems that distribute the products into weighing heads. This type of machine is suitable for large and heavy products. On the other hand, rotary multi-head weighing machines use a rotating disc to feed the products into multiple weighing heads simultaneously. This type is typically used for small and light products. Both types of machines offer high precision and efficiency in the packaging process.</p></p>
