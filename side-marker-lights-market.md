<p><p>Side marker lights are small, rectangular or circular lights typically located on the sides of vehicles. Side marker lights are typically amber or red in color and help to indicate the width and length of the vehicle to enhance safety on the road.</p><p>The global side marker lights market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.5% during the forecast period. LED side marker lights offer several advantages over traditional incandescent lights, such as lower energy consumption, longer lifespan, and better visibility. <p><p>The side marker lights market consists of two types: carbon filament bulb and LED. Carbon filament bulb side marker lights utilize a carbon filament to produce light when an electric current passes through it. These lights have been widely used for many years, providing a traditional and warm lighting appearance. On the other hand, LED side marker lights have gained popularity due to their energy efficiency, longer lifespan, and versatility in terms of design options. They emit light when an electric current flows through a semiconductor material, making them a modern and sustainable choice.</p></p>
