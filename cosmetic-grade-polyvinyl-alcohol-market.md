<p><ul><li>Fully Hydrolized</li><li>Partially Hydrolized</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cosmetic Grade Polyvinyl Alcohol (PVA) is available in two types: fully hydrolized and partially hydrolized. Fully hydrolized PVA is a water-soluble polymer that has undergone complete hydrolysis, resulting in a high degree of water solubility. It is often used in cosmetic products for its excellent film-forming properties and moisture-retention abilities. Partially hydrolized PVA, on the other hand, has undergone partial hydrolysis and has lower water solubility. This type of PVA is typically used in personal care products as a thickening agent or binder.</p></p>
