<p><p>The organic tobacco market can be classified into four major types based on the curing process: flue cured, air cured, sun cured, and fire cured. Flue cured organic tobacco is dried using a controlled heat source, producing a mild flavor. Air cured tobacco is naturally dried indoors, resulting in a medium strength tobacco. Sun cured tobacco is dried outdoors under the sun, creating a rich and bold taste. Fire cured tobacco is hung over open fires, giving it a smoky and robust flavor. These different curing methods contribute to the distinct characteristics and preferences within the organic tobacco market.</p></p>
