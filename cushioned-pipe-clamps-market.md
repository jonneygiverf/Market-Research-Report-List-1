<p><p>Cushioned pipe clamps are available in two types, namely Type I and Type II. Type I pipe clamps are designed for standard pipe installations and offer basic support. They are commonly used in industrial applications where pipe vibrations and movements are minimal. On the other hand, Type II pipe clamps are specifically designed for heavy-duty pipe installations where high levels of vibration, movement, or external forces are expected. They provide maximum support, stability, and absorption of vibrations, ensuring the longevity and integrity of the pipes in demanding environments.</p></p>
