<p><p>A Flat Panel Detector (FPD) is a type of solid-state X-ray detector that is used in radiology imaging systems. <p><strong>The Flat Panel Detector (FPD) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>IndirectFPD</li><li>DirectFPD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flat panel detector (FPD) market is categorized into two types: indirect FPD and direct FPD. </p><p>Indirect FPD refers to a technology where X-ray photons are converted into light photons through a scintillator material, and then these light photons are further transformed into electrical signals using an array of amorphous silicon photodiodes. This method provides high-resolution images, making it suitable for digital radiography and fluoroscopy applications.</p><p>Direct FPD, on the other hand, directly converts X-ray photons into electrical signals using a photoconductor material, eliminating the need for light conversion. This results in high sensitivity and efficiency, making it ideal for demanding imaging applications such as CT scanners and mammography machines.</p></p>
