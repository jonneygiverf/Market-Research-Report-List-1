<p><p>Pets anti-infective medicine market can be categorized into two types: external use and internal use medications. External use medications are applied directly on the pet's skin, fur, or affected area to treat infections, wounds, or skin-related issues. They may include ointments, creams, sprays, or shampoos. On the other hand, internal use medications are given to pets by mouth or injection to treat internal infections, such as respiratory, urinary, or gastrointestinal infections. These medications are absorbed by the pet's body, reaching the bloodstream to fight off the infection.</p></p>
