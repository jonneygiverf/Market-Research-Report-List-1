<p><p>Apricot extract can be categorized into two types: bitter apricot extract and sweet apricot extract. Bitter apricot extract is derived from the bitter apricot variety and is known for its high amygdalin content, which converts to hydrogen cyanide in the body. This extract is commonly used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits. On the other hand, sweet apricot extract is obtained from the sweet apricot variety and is widely used in culinary applications and the production of jams, jellies, and other sweet treats due to its pleasant taste and aroma.</p></p>
