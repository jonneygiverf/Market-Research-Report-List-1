<p><p>The safety prefilled syringe market can be divided into two types based on the material used: glass-based and plastic-based. </p><p>The glass-based syringes are made of high-quality borosilicate glass, which is known for its strength and durability. These syringes have a lower risk of leaching chemicals into the medication and are preferred for drugs that are sensitive to light and temperature.</p><p>On the other hand, plastic-based syringes are made of medical-grade plastics like polypropylene. These syringes are lightweight and shatter-resistant, making them convenient for transportation and reducing the risk of injuries due to glass breakage. They are commonly used for drugs that are not sensitive to light and temperature variations.</p></p>
