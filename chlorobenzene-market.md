<p><p>Chlorobenzene is a chemical compound used in various industries. The market for chlorobenzene can be categorized into four types. Monochlorobenzene refers to chlorobenzene with a single chlorine atom, commonly used in the production of insecticides and dyes. Orthodichlorobenzene contains two chlorine atoms, mainly utilized as a solvent in the manufacturing of textiles and pesticides. Paradichlorobenzene, also with two chlorine atoms, has applications in the production of mothballs and deodorants. Lastly, the "Others" category includes various chlorobenzene derivatives used in pharmaceuticals, agrochemicals, and other specialized industries.</p></p>
