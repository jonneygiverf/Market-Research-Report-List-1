<p><p>Digital textile printing inks market offers various types of inks based on their chemical composition and application requirements. Dispersed inks are widely used for printing on polyester fibers due to their excellent color fastness. Reactive inks are commonly used for natural fibers like cotton, as they react with the fiber to create a strong bond. Acid inks are suitable for protein-based fibers like silk, while pigment inks work well on a variety of fabric types. Each type of ink caters to specific textile printing needs, ensuring vibrant and durable results.</p></p>
