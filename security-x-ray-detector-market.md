<p><p>The Security X-Ray Detector Market comprises two main types: Cabinet X-Ray Detectors and Portable X-Ray Systems. Cabinet X-Ray Detectors are fixed, immobile devices used in security checkpoints at locations like airports and government buildings. They efficiently screen large items such as luggage, packages, and cargo. On the other hand, Portable X-Ray Systems are compact and flexible tools that can be easily moved from one location to another.
