<p><p>The elastomeric roof coating market consists of two types of coatings: water-based paints and solvent-based paints. Water-based paints are made of a water-based emulsion that acts as a binder, resulting in a flexible and durable coating. They are environmentally friendly, easy to apply, and have low levels of volatile organic compounds. On the other hand, solvent-based paints contain solvents as a binder, which evaporate during the drying process. They offer excellent adhesion and resistance to extreme weather conditions but have higher levels of volatile organic compounds.</p></p>
