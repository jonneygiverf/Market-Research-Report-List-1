<p><p>The paper-based consumer bags market comprises two main types: laminated bags and non-laminated bags. On the other hand, non-laminated bags are made solely of paper and are typically used for lightweight items or as shopping bags. While laminated bags provide increased protection, non-laminated bags are more environmentally friendly and recyclable. Both types cater to different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
