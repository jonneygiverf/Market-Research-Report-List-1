Additionally, the rising disposable income and changing lifestyles of consumers are driving the demand for comfortable bedding and furniture, further fueling market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the packaging industry is adopting FPF as an efficient and eco-friendly alternative to traditional materials like wood, which is projected to contribute to market growth. The construction industry is also expected to provide opportunities for the flexible polyurethane foam market as it is used for insulation, sealing, and soundproofing in buildings.</p><p>Furthermore, the electronics industry is witnessing advancements in lightweight and energy-efficient products, creating a demand for FPF-based solutions.
