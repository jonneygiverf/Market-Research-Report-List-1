<p><p>An airliner seat refers to the seating arrangement in commercial aircraft designed to accommodate passengers during their air travel. The increasing air passenger traffic worldwide, coupled with the rising demand for comfortable seating options, has been a key driver of market growth. Airlines are increasingly focusing on reducing costs, including the price of seating options. This trend has led to the emergence of low-cost airlines, which prioritize affordable ticket prices over luxury seating options. As a result, seat manufacturers are investing in developing lightweight, durable, and cost-effective seats to cater to the needs of the low-cost carrier segment.</p><p>Another notable trend in the airliner seat market is the integration of advanced technologies.
