<p><p>The pipe coatings market comprises of two main types: liquid coatings and powder coatings. Liquid coatings are applied to pipes as a liquid solution and then dried, forming a protective layer. They are preferred for their ease of application and ability to cover complex pipe geometries. On the other hand, powder coatings are applied as a dry powder and then heated to form a protective layer. They are known for their durability and resistance to corrosion. Both types play a crucial role in protecting pipes from wear, corrosion, and external environmental factors.</p></p>
