<p><p>The fertilizer additive market consists of several types of additives that are used to enhance the performance and functionality of fertilizers. Anticaking agents prevent the formation of lumps and improve flowability. Dedusting agents help in reducing dust emissions during fertilizer handling. Antifoam agents are used to control foam formation during fertilizer production. Hydrophobic agents increase the water resistance of fertilizers. Corrosion inhibitors protect the equipment from corrosive effects of fertilizers. Other types of additives may include colorants, stabilizers, and pH adjusters that contribute to product stability and efficacy.</p></p>
