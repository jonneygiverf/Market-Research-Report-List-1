<p><p>Gallium Oxide (Ga2O3) has various market types for its production. Chemical synthesis involves the reaction of gallium compounds with an oxidizing agent. Thermal vaporization and sublimation method involve heating and subsequent cooling of gallium oxide to obtain the desired product. Chemical vapor deposition technique allows the controlled growth of Ga2O3 films on substrates. Molecular beam epitaxy involves the deposition of gallium oxide layers atom by atom.
