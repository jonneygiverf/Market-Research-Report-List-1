HFXLPE Insulation stands for Cross-Linked Polyethylene insulation, providing high thermal stability and resistance to moisture and abrasion. PVC Insulation refers to cables insulated with Polyvinyl Chloride, known for its durability, low cost, and resistance to oils and chemicals.
