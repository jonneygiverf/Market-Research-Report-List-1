<p><p>Nano titanium dioxide powder is used in various industries such as cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals. It is available in two types: rutile type and anatase type. Rutile type nano titanium dioxide powder has a higher refractive index, better UV absorption, and superior dispersibility. It is commonly used in sunscreens and coatings. Anatase type nano titanium dioxide powder has a lower refractive index and is more photocatalytic. It is primarily used in self-cleaning coatings and environmental applications. Both types have unique properties and applications, making them suitable for different market segments.</p></p>
