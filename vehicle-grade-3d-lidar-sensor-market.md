<p><p>The Vehicle Grade 3D LiDAR Sensor market consists of two main types: Mechanical LiDAR and Solid State LiDAR. Mechanical LiDAR sensors use rotating mirrors to scan their surroundings and measure distance, providing highly detailed and accurate 3D data. On the other hand, Solid State LiDAR sensors employ solid-state technology, eliminating the need for moving parts and offering a more compact and durable solution. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various applications such as autonomous vehicles and advanced driver-assistance systems.</p></p>
