<p><p>The hot glue gun stick market can be categorized into two main types: opaque and transparent. Opaque glue sticks are solid in color and cannot be seen through, making them ideal for projects where the glue lines need to be hidden. On the other hand, transparent glue sticks are see-through, allowing for more precise and visible application. The choice between the two types depends on the desired outcome of the project and the level of visibility required for the glue lines.</p></p>
