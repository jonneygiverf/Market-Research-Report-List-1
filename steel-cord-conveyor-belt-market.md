<p><p>Steel-cord conveyor belts are widely used in various industries for efficient material transportation. Type I belts have steel cords embedded in the rubber covers on both sides, providing excellent strength and impact resistance. They are suitable for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, Type II belts have additional steel cords arranged in a stepped pattern inside the belt, enhancing its transverse rigidity and preventing longitudinal tearing. They are ideal for long-distance applications and improve belt stability under high tensions and large conveying angles. Both types have distinct features catering to different industry needs.</p></p>
