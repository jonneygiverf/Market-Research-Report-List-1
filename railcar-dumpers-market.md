<p><p>Railcar dumpers are machines used to unload bulk materials from railcars. There are three main types of railcar dumpers: O-Type Rotary, C-Type Rotary, and Side-Discharge. O-Type Rotary dumpers consist of a stationary table and a rotating arm that flips the railcar sideways to empty its contents. C-Type Rotary dumpers have a rotating drum that rotates the railcar to empty it. Side-Discharge dumpers tilt the railcar to one side to dump the materials. These different types offer various advantages and are used in different industries depending on specific requirements.</p></p>
