<p><p>The electronic grade liquid silicone rubber (LSR) market consists of two types: single component LSR and two component LSR. Single component LSR is a self-bonding material that does not require mixing with any additional components. It is convenient to use and provides excellent adhesion properties. On the other hand, two component LSR requires mixing a base LSR with a curing agent before application. It offers flexibility in controlling the curing time and allows for precise customization of the material's properties. Both types are widely used in the electronics industry for various applications.</p></p>
