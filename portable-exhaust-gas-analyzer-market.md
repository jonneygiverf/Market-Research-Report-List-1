<p><p>The Portable Exhaust Gas Analyzer market offers various types of devices to measure exhaust gas emissions. The NDIR Absorption Method relies on the principle that different gases absorb infrared light at different wavelengths. The Electrochemical Method measures gas concentrations using chemical reactions. The Hydrogen Flame Ionization Method detects ionizable hydrocarbons by burning them with a hydrogen flame. The Chemiluminescence Method measures nitrogen oxide concentrations by chemically enhancing their luminescence. These technologies provide accurate and reliable methods for analyzing exhaust gas emissions in portable devices.</p></p>
