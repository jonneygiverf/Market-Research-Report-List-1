<p><p>The Coated Process Separator market types include ceramic coating, resin coating, PVDF coating, and others. Ceramic coating refers to the application of a thin layer of ceramic material, providing enhanced resistance to corrosion and wear. Resin coating involves the use of a resin material to create a protective layer on the separator, offering improved chemical resistance and durability. PVDF coating utilizes a polymer material called polyvinylidene fluoride, providing excellent chemical resistance and electrical insulation properties.
