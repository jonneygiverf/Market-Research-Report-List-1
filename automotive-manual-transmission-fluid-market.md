<p><p>The automotive manual transmission fluid market is categorized into three different types: GL-1 to GL-3, GL-4, and GL-5. GL-1 to GL-3 fluids are basic gear oils suitable for vintage vehicles. GL-4 oils are used in modern manual transmissions and offer improved protection to gears under various operating conditions. GL-5 fluids are specifically designed for vehicles with high torque or heavy-duty applications, providing superior protection and lubrication to gears. These different types of manual transmission fluids cater to different vehicle requirements, ensuring optimal performance and longevity of the transmission system.</p></p>
