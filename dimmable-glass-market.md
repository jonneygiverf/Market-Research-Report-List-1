<p><strong>The Dimmable Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PDLC Glass</li><li>Electrochromic Glass</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dimmable glass market includes various types of glass that can adjust their transparency, allowing control over the amount of light passing through. PDLC (Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal) glass incorporates liquid crystals that rearrange when electric current is applied, making it opaque or transparent. Electrochromic glass changes its opacity when voltage is applied, using a thin coating of conductive metal oxides. Additionally, there are other types in the market, such as suspended particle device (SPD) glass, which uses particles to control light transmission. These technologies offer innovative solutions for privacy, energy efficiency, and comfort in various applications.</p></p>
