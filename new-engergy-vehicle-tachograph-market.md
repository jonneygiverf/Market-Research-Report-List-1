<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle Tachograph market can be divided into two types: Integrated and Portable. </p><p>Integrated tachographs are built into the vehicle's dashboard and are permanently installed. They provide real-time monitoring and recording of various vehicle data, such as speed, distance traveled, and driver behavior. These tachographs are typically used in commercial fleets to ensure compliance with regulations and improve safety.</p><p>Portable tachographs, on the other hand, are compact devices that can be easily installed and removed from vehicles. They offer similar functionalities to integrated tachographs but are more flexible and can be utilized in a wider range of vehicles. Portable tachographs are commonly used by individual drivers or smaller businesses that require occasional tracking and monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
