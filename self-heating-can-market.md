<p><p>A self-heating can is a specialized container designed to heat its contents without the need for an external heat source. It typically consists of two compartments separated by a membrane, with one compartment containing a reactive substance that generates heat upon contact with the substance in the other compartment. This technology provides convenience and efficiency, making it attractive for various applications, such as ready-to-eat meals, beverages, and medical products.</p><p>The self-heating can market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue expanding in the future. Additionally, technological advancements have improved the efficiency and safety of self-heating cans, further boosting their popularity.</p><p>Moreover, the market's growth is fueled by the expanding working population, rising disposable incomes, and changing lifestyles, particularly in urban areas.
