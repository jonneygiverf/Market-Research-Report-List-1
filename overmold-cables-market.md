<p><p>Overmold cables are available in various types of materials including rubber, ABS plastic, PVC, PP, and PE. Rubber overmold cables are known for their flexibility, durability, and resistance to extreme temperatures and chemicals. ABS plastic overmold cables offer excellent mechanical strength and impact resistance. PVC overmold cables are highly versatile, providing good electrical insulation and protection against moisture and abrasion. PP overmold cables have high tensile strength and resistance to chemicals and UV radiation. PE overmold cables are commonly used for outdoor applications due to their exceptional weather resistance and electrical insulation properties.</p></p>
