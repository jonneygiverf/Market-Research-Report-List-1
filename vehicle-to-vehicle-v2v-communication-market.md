<p><p>The Vehicle-To-Vehicle (V2V) Communication Market consists of two types: Cellular Based Technology and Dedicated Short Range Communication (DSRC) Market. Cellular Based Technology utilizes existing cellular networks to enable communication between vehicles, allowing for real-time exchange of information like speed, location, and traffic conditions. On the other hand, DSRC Market uses a dedicated short-range communication system using a specific frequency band, facilitating direct communication between nearby vehicles without relying on cellular networks. Both types aim to improve road safety and enhance the efficiency of transportation systems.</p></p>
