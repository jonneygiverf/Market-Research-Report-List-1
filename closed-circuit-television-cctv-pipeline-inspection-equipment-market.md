These advancements enable automated analysis of the captured footage, improving the accuracy and speed of detecting pipeline defects.</p><p>In conclusion, the CCTV pipeline inspection equipment market is expected to thrive in the coming years due to increasing infrastructure maintenance needs, stringent safety regulations, and technological advancements.
