These bands come in various thicknesses, including thin, medium, and thick. Thin edge bands are typically around 0.4mm in thickness and are used for lighter applications like shelving. Medium edge bands are around 1mm thick and are commonly used for furniture board edges. Thick edge bands, with a thickness of around 2mm, are suitable for heavy-duty applications such as countertops or table edges. The different thicknesses cater to various needs and usage requirements in the furniture industry.</p></p>
