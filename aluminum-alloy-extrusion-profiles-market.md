<p><p>The aluminum alloy extrusion profiles market offers three main types: forward extrusion, backward extrusion, and composite extrusion. Forward extrusion involves pushing the aluminum material through a die to create the desired shape. Backward extrusion involves pulling the material through the die in the opposite direction. Composite extrusion combines two or more materials to create a profile with enhanced properties. These processes are widely used in various industries for manufacturing aluminum products due to their versatility and ability to produce complex shapes.</p></p>
