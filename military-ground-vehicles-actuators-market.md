<p><p>The Military Ground Vehicles Actuators market comprises two main types: linear and rotary actuators. Linear actuators are devices that convert rotational motion into linear motion, allowing for straight line movement. These actuators are commonly used in military ground vehicles to control various components like doors, ramps, or steering mechanisms. On the other hand, rotary actuators enable rotational motion in a controlled manner, helping with the movement of wheels, weapons systems, or turrets in military ground vehicles. Both types are essential for the efficient functionality and maneuverability of military ground vehicles.</p></p>
