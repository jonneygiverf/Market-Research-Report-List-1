<p><p>A grating ruler, also known as an optical linear encoder, is a device commonly used in motion control systems for accurate measurement and feedback. It consists of a glass or metal scale with fine lines, also known as gratings, and a readhead that moves along the scale to generate position data. The grating ruler translates linear displacements into electrical signals, enabling precise positioning and motion control in various applications, including industrial machines, robotics, and metrology.</p><p>The grating ruler market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years. The expanding automation industry, increasing demand for high-precision motion control systems, and the need for accurate measurement in various industries are driving the market growth.
