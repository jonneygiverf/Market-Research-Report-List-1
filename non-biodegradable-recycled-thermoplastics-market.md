<p><p>The non-biodegradable recycled thermoplastics market consists of three main types: flakes, pellets, and granulates. Flakes are small pieces of thermoplastics obtained through the recycling process, which can be further processed into new products. Pellets are small pellets or beads made from melted and reformed thermoplastics, which are then used in various manufacturing processes. Lastly, granulates are sand-sized particles of thermoplastics, usually used as fillers in industrial applications. These different forms allow for versatility and ease of use in different industries, contributing to the growth of the non-biodegradable recycled thermoplastics market.</p></p>
