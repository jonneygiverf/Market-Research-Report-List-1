<p><p>The polyethylene pellets market consists of various types such as HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene), LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene), LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene), and others. HDPE pellets are known for their high strength, stiffness, and toughness, making them suitable for applications like pipes, bottles, and containers. LLDPE pellets possess excellent flexibility, impact resistance, and heat sealability, often used in packaging films, household products, and automotive parts. LDPE pellets have good flexibility and are commonly used in applications like squeeze bottles and agricultural films. The "others" category includes polyethylene pellets with unique properties or specific applications, which are not covered by the other types.</p></p>
