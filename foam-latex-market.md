<p><p>The foam latex market is divided into two main types: natural rubber latex and synthetic rubber latex. Natural rubber latex is derived from the sap of rubber trees and contains proteins that can cause allergic reactions. It is commonly used in bedding products, medical gloves, and consumer goods. Synthetic rubber latex, on the other hand, is artificially created and does not contain the proteins found in natural latex, making it an allergen-free alternative. It is utilized in various applications including foam mattresses, cushions, and footwear. Both types of foam latex have their own advantages and uses in different industries.</p></p>
