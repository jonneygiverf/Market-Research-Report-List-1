<p><p>Refined cotton is categorized into two market types: industrial grade and food grade. Industrial grade refined cotton is primarily used for non-consumable purposes, such as in the production of textiles, fabrics, and industrial machinery. It undergoes extensive processing to remove impurities and is considered suitable for industrial applications. On the other hand, food grade refined cotton is specifically processed and purified to meet rigorous safety and quality standards for consumption. It is commonly used in the food and beverage industry for applications like filtration and packaging. Both market types cater to different industries and their respective requirements.</p></p>
