<p><p>A gangway for railway vehicles is a passage that allows movement between two adjacent railcars or compartments. The gangway also enables the exchange of goods and supplies between railcars during transportation.</p><p>The gangway for railway vehicles market is expected to grow at a significant rate in the coming years. The increasing focus on passenger safety, along with the need for efficient and seamless train operations, is driving the demand for advanced gangway systems. The rising adoption of high-speed trains and the growing awareness about the benefits of gangway-connected railcars are further fueling the market growth.</p><p>Additionally, the integration of smart technologies in gangway systems is providing new growth opportunities in the market. Both types serve the purpose of connecting railway vehicles smoothly, ensuring convenience and safety for passengers during transit.</p></p>
