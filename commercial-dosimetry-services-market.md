<p><p>Commercial dosimetry services market refers to the industry that provides radiation monitoring solutions to various sectors, including medical, nuclear, industrial, and research. The market can be classified into three main types based on the technology used. OSL (Optically Stimulated Luminescence) technology involves the use of special crystals that can measure radiation exposure. TLD (Thermoluminescent Dosimeters) technology uses small crystals that emit light when heated, providing data on radiation levels. CR-39 (Chemical Etched Track Detector) technology utilizes specially designed plastic detectors to measure radiation accurately.</p></p>
