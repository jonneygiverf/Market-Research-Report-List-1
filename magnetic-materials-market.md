<p><ul><li>Soft Magnetic Material</li><li>Permanent Magnetic Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnetic materials market can be classified into two major types: soft magnetic materials and permanent magnetic materials. Soft magnetic materials are characterized by easy magnetization and demagnetization abilities, making them suitable for applications where frequent magnetic field changes are required, such as transformers and inductors. On the other hand, permanent magnetic materials retain their magnetism even in the absence of an external magnetic field, making them ideal for applications such as electric motors and magnetic separators. These two market types cater to different industries and play vital roles in various technological applications.</p></p>
