<p><p>Variable Valve Timing (VVT) systems are used in internal combustion engines to optimize performance, improve fuel efficiency, and reduce emissions. Cam-Phasing System is a type of VVT that adjusts the position of the camshaft to modify valve timing based on engine operating conditions. This allows for better control of valve opening and closing times, resulting in improved power and efficiency. Variable Valve Actuation Systems, on the other hand, go a step further by enabling independent control of valve lift, duration, and timing. This advanced technology provides even greater control over engine performance, enhancing power output and efficiency.</p></p>
