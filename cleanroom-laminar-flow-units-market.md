<p><p>Cleanroom laminar flow units are used in environments that require a high level of cleanliness and contamination control, such as laboratories and pharmaceutical manufacturing facilities. There are two types of cleanroom laminar flow units: vertical flow units and horizontal flow units. Vertical flow units release filtered air downward in a vertical direction, while horizontal flow units release filtered air horizontally. Both types help to maintain a sterile and controlled environment, but the choice between vertical and horizontal flow depends on the specific needs and requirements of the cleanroom.</p></p>
