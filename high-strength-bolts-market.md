<p><p>High Strength Bolts Market Types refer to the different variations of bolts available in the market. Hexagon Bolts, also known as hex bolts, have a hexagonal head and are commonly used in construction and machinery. Buttonheads bolts have a rounded head with a low profile, suitable for applications that require a smooth finish. Coach Bolts, also called carriage bolts, have a domed or countersunk head, commonly used in wood connections. Plow bolts have a flat countersunk head and are commonly used in agricultural applications. Other market types include specialty bolts designed for specific purposes or industries.</p></p>
