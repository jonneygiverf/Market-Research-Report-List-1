<p><p>The Aircraft Braking Systems market consists of various types, including Aircraft Disc Brakes, Thrust Reversers, and Air Brakes. Aircraft Disc Brakes are responsible for stopping the aircraft during landing by applying frictional force to the rotating wheels. Thrust Reversers redirect the aircraft engine's flow to reverse thrust, aiding in deceleration upon landing. Air Brakes control the aircraft's speed and descent during landing by utilizing air pressure. These braking systems play a crucial role in ensuring safe and efficient aircraft operations.</p></p>
