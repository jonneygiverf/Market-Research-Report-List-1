<p><p>The hydrogenated rosin market is categorized into three types based on the softening point. Hydrogenated rosin with a softening point between 100℃ and 135℃ finds application in the ink and coating industry due to its moderate softening point, allowing for better viscosity control. Hydrogenated rosin with a softening point above 135℃ is preferred in the electronics industry as it offers high heat resistance and stability.</p></p>
