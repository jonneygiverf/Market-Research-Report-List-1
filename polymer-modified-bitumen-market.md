Plastomer Modified Bitumen is PMB that contains plastomers, offering increased flexibility and resistance to deformation. Crumb Rubber Modified Bitumen is PMB that incorporates crumb rubber, delivering improved performance in terms of durability and road noise reduction. Lastly, the "Others" category includes any other types of PMB that do not fall under the mentioned classifications.</p></p>
