<p><p>Textile colorants are available in various forms, including powder, granules, paste, and liquid. Powder colorants are finely ground particles that can be easily mixed into dyes or printing pastes. Granules are larger, uniform particles that offer better flow and dispersion properties. Paste colorants are thick, concentrated formulations that can be directly added to textile printing pastes. Liquid colorants are pre-dissolved formulations that offer convenience and ease of use. Each type of colorant offers distinct advantages and is chosen based on the specific application requirements and preferences in the textile industry.</p></p>
