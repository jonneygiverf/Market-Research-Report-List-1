<p><p>A marine boiler is a device that produces steam by heating water for use in various marine propulsion and auxiliary systems. It is an essential component of a ship's power generation and propulsion system. A steam turbine generator, on the other hand, is a device that converts the energy from high-pressure steam into mechanical energy, which drives an electrical generator to produce electricity.</p><p>The marine boiler and steam turbine generator market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for energy-efficient propulsion systems in the marine industry. The adoption of strict emission standards by various regulatory bodies is also driving the market growth, as marine boilers and steam turbine generators offer lower emissions compared to traditional systems.</p><p>Moreover, the growing maritime trade activities and the rising demand for ships with higher power generation capacities are fueling the demand for marine boilers and steam turbine generators. These systems offer benefits such as improved fuel efficiency, reduced maintenance costs, and compact design, which further contribute to their market growth.</p><p>The future outlook of the marine boiler and steam turbine generator market is favorable.
