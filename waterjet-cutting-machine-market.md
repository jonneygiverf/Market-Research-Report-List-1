<p><p>The Waterjet Cutting Machine market consists of two main types: Pure Waterjet Machines and Abrasive Waterjet Machines. Pure Waterjet Machines use only high-pressure water to cut through materials like foam, rubber, and softer metals. On the other hand, Abrasive Waterjet Machines mix pressurized water with abrasive materials to cut harder substances such as granite, steel, and ceramics. Each machine type offers unique benefits and applications, catering to different industries and cutting requirements. Both Pure Waterjet and Abrasive Waterjet Machines provide precise, efficient, and versatile cutting capabilities.</p></p>
