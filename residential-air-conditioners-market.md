<p><p>The residential air conditioners market consists of several types of components. The compressor is responsible for pumping the refrigerant throughout the system. The condenser helps in removing heat from the refrigerant by converting it into a high-pressure gas. The metering device regulates the flow of refrigerant into the cooling coil, where it absorbs heat from the air. The air handling unit then distributes the cooled air throughout the space via a duct system. Together, these components ensure efficient cooling and comfortable living environments.</p></p>
