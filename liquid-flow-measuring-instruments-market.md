<p><p>The liquid flow measuring instruments market is categorized into various types based on their functioning. Velocity flowmeters measure the velocity of the flowing liquid to determine the flow rate. Differential pressure flowmeters calculate flow by measuring the difference in pressure across a restriction in the flow path. Volume flowmeters directly measure the volume of the liquid passing through a pipe or channel. Open channel flowmeters are used for measuring the flow in open channels like rivers or canals. Other markets encompass niche flow measurement technologies or unique applications within the broader liquid flow measuring instruments market.</p></p>
