<p><p>Polysulfides are a class of chemicals widely used in various industries. In the market, they can be categorized into three types: sealants, adhesives, and others. Sealants refer to polysulfide-based materials used to create airtight and watertight seals in various applications such as construction, automotive, and aerospace. Adhesives, on the other hand, are polysulfide-based products that provide strong bonding properties, often used in industries like manufacturing, electronics, and healthcare. Lastly, the "others" category encompasses polysulfide-based products used for purposes other than sealing or bonding, such as coatings, insulation, and lubricants.</p></p>
