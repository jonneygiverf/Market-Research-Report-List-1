<p><p>Vertical booster pumps are specially designed pumps that are used to increase the water pressure in a water distribution system. Vertical booster pumps are typically installed in vertical positions, with the inlet and outlet ports positioned vertically.</p><p>The future outlook of the vertical booster pumps market looks promising.
