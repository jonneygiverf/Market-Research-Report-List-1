<p><p>The recycled polyester staple fibre market consists of two main types: solid fiber and hollow fiber. Solid fiber refers to polyester fibers that are uniform in thickness throughout their length. These fibers are commonly used in applications such as apparel, upholstery, and home textiles. On the other hand, hollow fiber refers to polyester fibers that have empty spaces or voids within them. These fibers are known for their lightweight and insulation properties, making them ideal for applications such as bedding, pillows, and thermal garments.</p></p>
