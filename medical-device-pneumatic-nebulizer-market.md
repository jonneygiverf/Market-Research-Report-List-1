<p><p>The medical device pneumatic nebulizer market consists of two main types: vented pneumatic nebulizers and breath-actuated pneumatic nebulizers. Vented pneumatic nebulizers are designed to deliver medication continuously by providing a continuous flow of air to create aerosol particles for inhalation. On the other hand, breath-actuated pneumatic nebulizers are triggered by the patient's inhalation, delivering medication only when the patient breathes in. These devices provide a more efficient and targeted drug delivery method than traditional vented pneumatic nebulizers.</p></p>
