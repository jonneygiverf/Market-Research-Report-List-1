<p><p>An anti-fog cloth is a specially designed cloth used to prevent fogging on various surfaces, such as eyeglasses, camera lenses, mirrors, windshields, and goggles. The cloth is generally made from a soft and lint-free material, ensuring safe and effective cleaning without scratching or damaging the surface.</p><p>The anti-fog cloth market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand from various end-use industries. The rise in disposable income and changing consumer lifestyle have led to a surge in the usage of eyewear, driving the demand for anti-fog cloths for eyeglasses.
