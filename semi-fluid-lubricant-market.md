<p><p>The semi-fluid lubricant market can be classified into three types: mineral oil-based, semisynthetic oil-based, and others. Mineral oil-based lubricants are derived from crude oil and are commonly used due to their good thermal stability and low cost. Semisynthetic oil-based lubricants are a blend of mineral oil and other synthetic fluids, offering enhanced performance and lubrication properties. The "others" category includes lubricants derived from vegetable oils or synthetic fluids, which are utilized in specific applications or industries where unique properties are required.</p></p>
