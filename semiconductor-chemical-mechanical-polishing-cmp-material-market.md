<p><strong>The Semiconductor Chemical-mechanical Polishing (CMP) Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CMP Pads</li><li>CMP Slurries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor Chemical-Mechanical Polishing (CMP) Material market consists of two main types: CMP Pads and CMP Slurries. CMP Pads are abrasion-resistant materials, usually made of polyurethane or polyvinyl acetate, used to planarize and polish the semiconductor surface. They help remove imperfections and ensure smoothness. On the other hand, CMP Slurries are chemical mixtures containing abrasive particles suspended in a liquid. They facilitate the polishing action by chemically interacting with the surface and enhancing material removal. Both CMP pads and slurries play vital roles in the semiconductor industry, helping to achieve high-precision and quality finishes.</p></p>
