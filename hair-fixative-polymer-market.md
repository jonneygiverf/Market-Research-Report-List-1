<p><p>The hair fixative polymer market is categorized into four types: anionic polymers, cationic polymers, non-ionic polymers, and amphoteric polymers. Anionic polymers are negatively charged and enhance the volume and texture of hair. Cationic polymers have a positive charge and provide conditioning and manageability to hair. Non-ionic polymers are neutral and improve flexibility and hold. Amphoteric polymers have both positive and negative charges, offering a wide range of benefits like volumizing, conditioning, and holding properties. These polymers are used in various hair care products to achieve desired styling effects.</p></p>
