<p><p>The Elevator Proximity Sensors Market includes two main types: Multi-beam Door Sensors and Ultrasonic Door Sensors. Multi-beam Door Sensors are designed to detect objects or people in the elevator doorway by emitting multiple beams of light and detecting interruptions in those beams. On the other hand, Ultrasonic Door Sensors utilize sound waves to detect objects or individuals in the pathway of an elevator door. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in enhancing safety and efficiency within elevator systems.</p></p>
