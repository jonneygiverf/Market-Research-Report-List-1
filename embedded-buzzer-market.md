<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Type</li><li>Electromagnetic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The embedded buzzer market comprises two main types: piezoelectric and electromagnetic. Piezoelectric buzzers use a piezoelectric element that produces sound when an electric field is applied to it. They are commonly used in small, portable devices due to their compact size and low power consumption. On the other hand, electromagnetic buzzers consist of a coil and a diaphragm that vibrate to generate sound when an electric current passes through the coil. These buzzers are often utilized in larger electronic products such as appliances and industrial equipment.</p></p>
