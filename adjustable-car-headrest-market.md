<p><p>The adjustable car headrest market offers three main types of headrests - 2-Way, 4-Way, and 6-Way. A 2-Way headrest can be adjusted vertically to provide comfort and support for the neck and head. A 4-Way headrest offers both vertical and horizontal adjustments, allowing for a more customized sitting position. Lastly, a 6-Way headrest provides additional adjustments, including forward and backward tilting, ensuring optimal comfort and reducing the risk of neck strain during long car rides. These various options cater to different individuals' preferences and enhance the overall driving experience.</p></p>
