<p><p>Paint and coatings market consists of various types, such as solvent-based coatings, waterborne coatings, high-solids coatings, powder coatings, and others. Solvent-based coatings are made with organic solvents, providing good adhesion and long-lasting protection. Waterborne coatings use water as a solvent, making them more environmentally friendly. High-solids coatings have minimal volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and offer excellent durability. Powder coatings are applied as dry powder and cured using heat, resulting in a hard, colorful finish. Other coatings include radiation-curable coatings, specialty coatings, and nanocoatings, catering to specific applications and functionalities.</p></p>
