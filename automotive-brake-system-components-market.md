<p><p>The automotive brake system is a crucial component of any vehicle as it is responsible for ensuring the safe stopping of the vehicle. EBS offer better control and improved safety features compared to conventional braking systems. The integration of electronic sensors and advanced technologies allows for quicker response times and better braking performance.</p><p>Another notable trend is the growing demand for lightweight brake components. Vehicle manufacturers are focusing on reducing the weight of vehicles to improve fuel efficiency. This has led to the development of lightweight brake systems that offer similar performance while reducing weight.</p><p>Furthermore, there is a rising demand for electric vehicles (EVs) and hybrid vehicles, which is driving the need for specialized brake systems. EVs and hybrids require regenerative braking systems that can recover and store energy during braking. This has led to the development of advanced regenerative braking systems to cater to the specific needs of these vehicles.</p><p>Overall, the automotive brake system and components market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for vehicles, safety regulations, and technological advancements.
