<p><p>PVC conduit pipes are a type of plastic tube used for electrical wiring installations. Rigid conduit pipes are widely used in commercial and industrial applications, while flexible conduit pipes are preferred in residential applications due to their ability to bend and adapt to difficult installation areas.</p><p>In terms of geography, Asia Pacific is expected to dominate the PVC conduit pipes market during the forecast period. <p><p>The PVC conduit pipes market includes two main types: flexible and rigid. Flexible PVC conduit pipes are designed to bend easily and are commonly used in situations that require flexibility, such as when navigating corners or obstacles. Rigid PVC conduit pipes, on the other hand, are inflexible and provide more protection to the electrical wiring inside. The choice between flexible and rigid PVC conduit pipes depends on the specific installation requirements and the level of flexibility and protection needed for the electrical wiring project.</p></p>
