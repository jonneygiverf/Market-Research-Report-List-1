<p><p>Electronic Sow Feeding (ESF) System is a technology that automates the process of feeding pregnant sows in pig farms. These devices are used to identify each sow and administer the appropriate amount of feed based on its specific nutritional requirements.</p><p>The ESF system eliminates the need for manual feeding and allows for customized feeding plans to improve sow health and productivity. It also optimizes feed usage, reduces labor costs, and enhances animal welfare by providing a stress-free feeding environment.</p><p>The future of the ESF system market looks promising.
