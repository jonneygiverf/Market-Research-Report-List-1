PFA tubes offer enhanced transparency and flexibility, making them suitable for applications in the medical and chemical industries. FEP tubes have similar characteristics to PTFE tubes but are less expensive, making them a cost-effective option for many industries. ETFE tubes are known for their exceptional chemical resistance and are often used in harsh environments.</p></p>
