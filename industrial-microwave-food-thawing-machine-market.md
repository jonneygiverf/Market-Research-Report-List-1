<p><p>The Industrial Microwave Food Thawing Machine market comprises several types, including the continuously type, belt type, and others. Continuously type thawing machines are designed to thaw food products continuously by passing them through a microwave heating chamber. Belt type machines, on the other hand, use conveyor belts to transport food products through the microwave chamber, ensuring an efficient thawing process. Lastly, the "others" segment encompasses different types of thawing machines that do not fall under the continuously or belt types, providing additional options for thawing food products in industrial settings.</p></p>
