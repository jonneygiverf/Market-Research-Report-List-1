They offer several advantages such as high power output, high efficiency, and compact size, making them suitable for applications like laser cutting, welding, and marking.</p><p>Gas Lasers: Gas lasers use a gas medium, such as helium-neon (He-Ne) or carbon dioxide (CO2), to generate coherent light. They are used in applications such as scientific research, telecommunications, and laser surgery.</p><p>Dye Lasers: Dye lasers use an organic dye as the gain medium.
