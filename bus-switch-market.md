<p><p>The bus switch market consists of two main types: touch type and press type. Touch type bus switches are designed to be activated simply by touching or hovering over them, without any physical pressure or force applied. These switches are sensitive to slight movements and are often used in applications where ease of use and precision are important. Press type bus switches, on the other hand, require physical pressure to be applied in order to activate. These switches are generally more robust and are commonly used in situations where durability and resistance to accidental activation are critical.</p></p>
