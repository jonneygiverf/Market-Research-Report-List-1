<p><p>The kick scooter tire market consists of two types: solid tires and pneumatic tires. Solid tires are made of a solid rubber material and are puncture-proof. They require minimal maintenance and are ideal for a smooth ride on city streets. On the other hand, pneumatic tires are filled with air, providing better shock absorption and a more comfortable ride. However, they require regular maintenance to check and adjust air pressure and are more susceptible to punctures. Both types cater to different preferences and terrains for kick scooter riders.</p></p>
