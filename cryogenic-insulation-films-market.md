<p><p>Cryogenic insulation films are used to provide thermal insulation in low temperature applications. There are two types of cryogenic insulation films: single side coated and double side coated. Single side coated films have a layer of insulation material applied on one side of the film, while double side coated films have insulation material applied on both sides. These coatings enhance the film's ability to retain heat, making it suitable for cryogenic applications where maintaining low temperatures is crucial.</p></p>
