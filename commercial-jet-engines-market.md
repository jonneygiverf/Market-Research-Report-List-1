<p><p>The commercial jet engines market consists of three main types: turbojet engines, turbofan engines, and turboprop engines. Turbojet engines are the oldest type and work by compressing air and mixing it with fuel to create propulsion. Turbofan engines are the most commonly used today, as they have a larger and more efficient fan at the front that generates most of the thrust, while the inner core operates similarly to a turbojet engine. Turboprop engines are mainly used for smaller planes and have a propeller driven by a turbine engine, providing both propulsion and improved fuel efficiency.</p></p>
