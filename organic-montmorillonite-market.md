<p><p>The organic montmorillonite market comprises two main types: The High Viscosity Series and the Easy to Disperse Series. The High Viscosity Series is characterized by its ability to greatly enhance viscosity and thixotropy in various applications such as coatings, adhesives, and sealants. On the other hand, the Easy to Disperse Series is known for its excellent dispersibility and compatibility with different matrices.
