<p><p>The Oil and Gas Processing Seals market includes two main types of seals: single seal and double seal. Single seals are designed with a single barrier that separates the process fluid from the external environment, ensuring minimal leakage. On the other hand, double seals consist of two barriers, providing an additional layer of protection against leakage. Double seals are considered more reliable and are often used in high-pressure or critical applications. Both types of seals play a crucial role in maintaining the integrity and safety of oil and gas processing operations.</p></p>
