<p><p>The refrigeration insulation materials market comprises various types of insulation materials such as honeycomb glass, closed cell phenolic resin, flexible elastomer, polyisocyanurate, and polystyrene. Honeycomb glass insulation is made of glass fibers arranged in a honeycomb pattern, providing high strength and rigidity. Closed cell phenolic resin insulation is known for its excellent thermal insulation properties. Flexible elastomer insulation is a rubber-based material that offers good flexibility and resistance to moisture. Polyisocyanurate and polystyrene insulation are both lightweight and have low thermal conductivity, making them suitable for refrigeration applications.</p></p>
