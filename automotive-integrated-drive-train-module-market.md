<p><p>The Automotive Integrated Drive Train Module market consists of two main types: Electric System Module and Hybrid Power System Module. Electric System Module refers to a drive train module that is solely powered by electricity. It contains components such as electric motors, inverters, and batteries. On the other hand, Hybrid Power System Module combines both electric and combustion engine power sources, utilizing components like electric motors, batteries, and an internal combustion engine. Both modules play a crucial role in enhancing the efficiency and performance of automotive drive trains.</p></p>
