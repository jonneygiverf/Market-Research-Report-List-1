<p><p>The automatic washer disinfecting systems market comprises two main types: fully-automatic and semi-automatic systems. Fully-automatic systems are designed to perform the entire washing and disinfection process without any manual intervention. These systems typically include advanced features like automatic dosage of disinfectants, temperature control, and programmable settings. On the other hand, semi-automatic systems require some level of manual intervention, such as loading and unloading of laundry and adjusting settings. While both types serve the purpose of disinfecting laundry, fully-automatic systems offer a more convenient and hands-free experience.</p></p>
