<p><p>The Industrial Metallic Enclosure market consists of two main types: wall-mounted enclosures and freestanding/floormounted enclosures. Wall-mounted enclosures are designed to be attached to the walls, providing a space-saving solution for housing equipment and electrical components. On the other hand, freestanding/floormounted enclosures are stand-alone structures that can be placed on the floor. These enclosures offer more space and flexibility for larger equipment and have the advantage of being easily accessible from all sides. Both types of enclosures are widely used in various industries to ensure the protection and organization of equipment and components.</p></p>
