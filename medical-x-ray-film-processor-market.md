<p><p>The medical X-ray film processor market consists of two main types: semi-automatic film processors and fully automatic film processors. Semi-automatic film processors require some manual intervention by the user. They involve loading the film manually, setting up the processing parameters, and unloading the film after processing. On the other hand, fully automatic film processors operate without manual intervention. They have automated mechanisms for film loading, processing, and unloading, requiring minimal user involvement. Both types of processors are widely used in medical facilities for developing X-ray films efficiently and accurately.</p></p>
