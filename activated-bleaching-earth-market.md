<p><strong>The Activated Bleaching Earth Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>The Wet Technology</li><li>The Dry Technology</li><li>The Vapour-phase Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Activated Bleaching Earth market comprises three major types: the Wet Technology, the Dry Technology, and the Vapor-phase Technology. In the Wet Technology, the bleaching earth is mixed with water to form a slurry, which is then used to remove impurities from oils and fats. The Dry Technology involves mixing the bleaching earth with oil or fat directly, without the addition of water. Finally, the Vapor-phase Technology utilizes a continuous process where the oil or fat is passed through a bed of bleaching earth particles suspended in the vapor phase to achieve purification.</p></p>
