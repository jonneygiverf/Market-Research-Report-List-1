<p><p>Liquid caramel color is used as a food coloring agent and has different market types. Plain caramel color is made by heating and caramelizing sugar, resulting in a dark color. Caustic sulfite caramel color is produced using a combination of sugar and sulfur dioxide, creating a lighter shade. Ammonia caramel color is made by adding ammonia to create a deep brown color, while ammonia sulfite caramel color is a blend of sulfur dioxide and ammonia, resulting in a highly stable and uniform color. These types cater to various food and beverage industry needs for enhancing appearance.</p></p>
