<p><p>The industrial grade magnesium hydroxide market consists of two types: the chemical synthesis method and the physical method. In the chemical synthesis method, magnesium hydroxide is produced through a chemical reaction between magnesium chloride and an alkali solution. This method allows for precise control over the product's purity and particle size distribution. On the other hand, the physical method involves directly extracting magnesium hydroxide from mineral sources. This method is less precise but relies on the natural occurrence of magnesium hydroxide. Both methods cater to different market requirements and applications.</p></p>
