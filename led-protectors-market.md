<p><strong>The LED Protectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shunt Protectors</li><li>Surge Protectors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LED protectors are devices designed to safeguard LED lighting systems from damage caused by electrical surges or other electrical issues. Shunt protectors are typically used to divert excessive current away from the LED circuit, protecting it from damage. Surge protectors, on the other hand, are designed to absorb and dissipate high-voltage spikes, preventing them from reaching the LED. The "Others" category encompasses various types of LED protectors, such as thermal protectors that prevent overheating, voltage regulators that stabilize the electrical supply, or polarity protectors that safeguard against reverse polarity connections.</p></p>
