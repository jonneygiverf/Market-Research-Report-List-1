<p><p>The single-phase meter market includes two main types: electromechanical type induction meters and electronic energy meters. Electromechanical type induction meters are traditional meters that use the mechanical rotation of an aluminum disc to measure electric energy consumption. On the other hand, electronic energy meters are advanced devices that use digital technology to measure energy consumption. These meters have more accuracy, display features, and are programmable for different tariff structures. The market for electronic energy meters has been growing rapidly due to their technological advancements and ability to provide more detailed energy consumption data.</p></p>
