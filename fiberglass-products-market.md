<p><p>The fiberglass products market consists of various types, including rovings and yarns. Rovings are strands of fiberglass used for reinforcement purposes in industries such as construction and automotive. They provide additional strength and durability to the final product. On the other hand, yarns are twisted strands of rovings used for weaving or knitting fabrics. They are widely used in applications such as insulation materials and textiles. Both rovings and yarns play a vital role in enhancing the performance and quality of fiberglass products in different industries.</p></p>
