<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Insect Growth Regulator (IGR) Bait market consists of three main types: Chitin Synthesis Inhibitors, Juvenile Hormone Analogs & Mimics, and Anti-Juvenile Hormone Agents. Chitin Synthesis Inhibitors hinder the formation of chitin, a crucial component of insect exoskeletons, leading to abnormal moulting and growth inhibition. Juvenile Hormone Analogs & Mimics imitate the hormones responsible for insect development, causing premature metamorphosis or hindering reproduction. Anti-Juvenile Hormone Agents disrupt the natural hormonal balance in insects, preventing their growth and development. These different types of IGR Baits offer effective methods for controlling insect populations by interfering with their growth and development processes.</p></p>
