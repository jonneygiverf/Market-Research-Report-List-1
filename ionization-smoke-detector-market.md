<p><p>The ionization smoke detector market can be categorized into two types: wireless smoke detectors and wired smoke detectors. Wireless smoke detectors are equipped with wireless technology, allowing them to communicate with a central control panel or other devices without the need for physical wiring. They offer flexibility in installation and can be easily relocated. On the other hand, wired smoke detectors are connected to the electrical system of a building through wires. They provide a reliable and constant power source, ensuring uninterrupted operation. Both types serve the purpose of detecting smoke and alerting occupants of potential fire hazards.</p></p>
