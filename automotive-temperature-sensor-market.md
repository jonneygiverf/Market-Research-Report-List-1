<p><strong>The Automotive Temperature Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermistor Temperature Sensor</li><li>Thermocouple Temperature Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive temperature sensor market is divided into two main types: thermistor temperature sensors and thermocouple temperature sensors. Thermistor sensors detect temperature changes by measuring electrical resistance, which varies with temperature. They are widely used in automotive applications due to their low cost and high accuracy. On the other hand, thermocouple temperature sensors measure temperature through the voltage generated at the junction of two different metals. They are popular for their durability and ability to measure a wide temperature range, making them suitable for harsh automotive environments.</p></p>
